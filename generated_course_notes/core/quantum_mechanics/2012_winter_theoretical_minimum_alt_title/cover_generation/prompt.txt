Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a lecture-note book on quantum mechanics from the Theoretical Minimum course.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with scientific elegance
- one clear quantum motif: wave packet, probability density layers, operator geometry, or coherent phase structure
- crisp linework with soft gradients, sparse geometry, and restrained structure
- generous negative space in the upper region for later title and attribution overlays
- simpler is better than busy, keep it calm and readable

Composition:
- portrait composition, aspect ratio 2:3, full page
- balanced center placement with elegant margins
- no text, no labels, no watermark, no fake equations

Color palette:
- deep indigo, charcoal, muted cream, and restrained cyan accents

Avoid:
- noise, clutter, stock-photo cliches, distorted or unreadable symbols, fake textbook figures, and overcrowded plots.
