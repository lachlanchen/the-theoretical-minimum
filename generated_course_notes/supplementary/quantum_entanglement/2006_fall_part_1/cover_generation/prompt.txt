Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on quantum entanglement, part 1.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with quiet intellectual tension
- one entanglement idea only: paired states, mirrored structure, linked traces, or nonlocal correlation
- crisp sparse linework and clear spatial balance
- simpler is better than busy; avoid overdrawn curves
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- parchment, charcoal, deep blue, and one restrained teal or silver accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one clean relational structure, not a crowded collage

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- fake equations, noisy plotting, tangled lines, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
