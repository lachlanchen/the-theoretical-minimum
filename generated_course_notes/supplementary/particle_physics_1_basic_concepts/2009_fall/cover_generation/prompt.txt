Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on particle physics basic concepts.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with precise scientific clarity
- one simple particle-physics motif only: a clean interaction vertex, sparse scattering paths, detector rings, or controlled geometric traces
- crisp linework, legible spacing, and fewer plotted elements
- prefer strong geometry over cluttered scientific busyness
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- warm cream, charcoal, steel blue, and one muted amber accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one coherent central structure

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- dense detector clutter, fake equations, tangled linework, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
