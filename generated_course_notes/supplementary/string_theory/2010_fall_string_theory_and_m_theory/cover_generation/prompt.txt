Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on string theory and M-theory.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with abstract geometric elegance
- one string-theory idea only: sparse string-like curves, clean brane-like planes, or higher-dimensional surface deformation
- clearer lines, more negative space, fewer layered elements
- keep it refined and quiet rather than busy or cosmic
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- cream, charcoal, midnight blue, and one restrained violet, magenta, or gold accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one clean central visual metaphor

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- sci-fi poster clutter, fake equations, noisy line tangles, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
