Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on the Standard Model.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with structural clarity
- one Standard Model idea only: gauge symmetry structure, particle-family organization, or clean interaction geometry
- clear line hierarchy and sparse forms
- simpler is better than busy; avoid chart-like clutter
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- warm paper, graphite, deep blue, and one controlled crimson or gold accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one organized central motif, not many competing diagrams

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- dense charts, fake equations, tangled linework, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
