Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on the Higgs boson.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism, clean and clear
- one simple symmetry-breaking idea only: a field landscape, Mexican-hat potential, or elegant deformation around a central well
- clearer curves, fewer layers, fewer plotted lines
- prefer simplicity and strong silhouette over decorative complexity
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- cream, charcoal, deep green, soft gray, with one restrained gold accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one central visual concept, not collage clutter

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- fake equations, dense plotting noise, messy curves, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
