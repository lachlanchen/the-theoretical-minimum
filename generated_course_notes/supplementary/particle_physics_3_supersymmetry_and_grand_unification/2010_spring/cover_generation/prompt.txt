Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on supersymmetry and grand unification.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with austere high-energy elegance
- one unification motif only: branching symmetry, merging structures, or sparse high-energy geometric relations
- clean curves and lines with strong hierarchy
- avoid decorative density; clarity matters more than spectacle
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- cream, charcoal, midnight blue, and one restrained copper or muted violet accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- one calm central organization

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- fantasy-science aesthetics, fake equations, noisy line tangles, stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
