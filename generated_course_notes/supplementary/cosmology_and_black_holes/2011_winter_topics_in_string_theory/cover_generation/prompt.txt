Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note volume on cosmology, black holes, and topics in string theory.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with deep scientific seriousness
- one calm spacetime idea only: a horizon, warped geometry, cosmological curvature, or string-inspired surface structure
- clear large forms with sparse, elegant lines
- avoid overcrowding; fewer elements, more atmosphere, more clarity
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- midnight blue, charcoal, warm cream, and one restrained copper or muted magenta accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- central visual metaphor, not a collage of science symbols

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- star-field clutter, sci-fi poster aesthetics, noisy linework, fake equations, or dense plot tangles
- stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, watermarks, or 3D shadows
