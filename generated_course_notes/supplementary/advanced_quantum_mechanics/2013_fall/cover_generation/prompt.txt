Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note book on advanced quantum mechanics.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, and not a poster.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism with scientific elegance
- one clear quantum motif: spectral traces, interference structure, operator geometry, or controlled wave pattern
- crisp linework and luminous structure, but keep it sparse and legible
- simpler is better than busy; avoid too many plots or overlapping curves
- reserve generous quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- deep indigo, charcoal, parchment, soft gray, and a restrained cyan accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- clean central organization with strong visual hierarchy

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, glyphs, numbers, or pseudo-typography
- noisy scientific plotting, dense line tangles, fake equations, or clutter
- stock-photo clichés, lecture screenshots, YouTube aesthetics, watermarks, or 3D shadows
